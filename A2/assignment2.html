Calculating Resistance
Ohm's Law
Voltage(Volts) = Current(Amperes) x Resistence(Ohms)
Power = 5V
Vred = 1.8V (red LED is 1.8V)
I = 20mA = 0.02A
V = 5V - 1.8V = 3.2V
R = V/I = 3.2V/0.02A = 160(Ohms)
The resistor should be at least 160 ohm.
I Choose resistors with 220 Ohms, since it is the closest to 160 Ohms.
For the button, I chose a 1k resistor, because the button will work when there is current.
