Calculating Resistance for LEDs:
According to Ohm's Law,
Voltage(Volts) = Current(Amperes) x Resistence(Ohms)
Power = 5V
Vred = Vgreen = 1.8V (red and green LED are both 1.8V)
I = 20mA = 0.02A
V = 5V - 1.8V = 3.2V
R = V/I = 3.2V/0.02A = 160(Ohms)
The resistor should be at least 160 ohm.
I Choose resistors with 220 Ohms, since it is the closest to 160 Ohms.


Calculating Resistance for the Photoresistor:
The maximum current is 200mA, and the Power is 5V.
Vout = Vin * (R2/(R1+R2))
I = 200mA
V = 5V
R = I/R = 5V/200mA = 25 (Ohms)
I Choose resistors with 1K Ohms.
